Hello there!

I am the ELLIS–SimTech independent research group leader of the University of Stuttgart, Germany. I head the Information Systems Group associated to the AI institute. We develop tools to enable intelligent systems to extract, process, and act on information.

 

I am joining the center for applied math (CMAP) of the École Polytechnique de Paris next January.

If you are looking to work with me, I will soon be filling a PhD student position (applications reviews will begin February/March 2025). Read more on how to join my group here.
